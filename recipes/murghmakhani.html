Murgh Makhani aka Butter Chicken

Follow along, you stup

for 4 servings

  • 2 lb chicken breast or thigh
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon chili or cayenne pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 6 tablespoons butter, divided into 2 tablespoon chunks
  • 1 ½ cups (or one big) yellow onion, diced
  • 3 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cinnamon, 3 inch (8 cm) stick or 1 teaspoon of cinnamon powder
  • 14 oz tomato sauce (one can)
  • a bit of water, 50 to 100 ml
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • fresh cilantro, chopped for garnish
  • rice, for serving

Preparation:

  1. Marinate chicken breast in chili powder, turmeric, salt and pepper.
  2. Let it sit for 15 minutes.
  3. Brown chicken in 2 Tbsp. of butter, remove and set aside.
  4. Melt another 2 Tbsp. of butter over medium heat, add onions, garlic, garam masala, grated ginger, chili powder, ground cumin, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, salt and pepper. Cook until fragrant.
  5. Add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the water and cream, bring back to a simmer.
  7. Return the chicken and simmer covered for another 10 -15 minutes.
  8. Stir in the last 2 tbsp of butter and additional salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve garnished with cilantro on top of rice.
  10. Enjoy!
  11. Tips and tricks: use ketchup or a bit of sugar on the tomate sauce. Careful on the pepper. use half the butter for chicken thighs. Paprika is good for the "marinade".
