Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is an indian fast food consisting of a thick spicy vegetable gravy served with soft dinner rolls. Pav means "bread roll" and bhaji means "vegetable dish". It is the textile worker's meal!

ingredients

  • Pav (bread rolls)
  • mixed vegetables (carrots, cauliflower, bell peppers, beans, potatoes and frozen peas)
  • butter
  • pav bhaji masala

making pav bhaji masala

dry roast, cooldown, grind and mix these together:

  • 2 tablespoons coriander seeds
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 tablespoon cumin or jeera
  • 1 teaspoon pepper corn
  • 0,5 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 3 dried red chillis
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 3 to 4 cloves
  • 0,5 tablespoon amchur (dried mango powder)

Steps

  1. rinse, peel and cube about 2 larger potatoes (around 300g). Clean 1 cup cauliflower florets. Rinse 0,5 to 0,75 green peas. Add one medium carrot (half cut chopped).
  2. For making masala, chop 1 medium onion, 2 medium tomatoes, half bell pepper and 1 green chilli. Also make 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste. Keep all of these aside.
  3. Add potatoes, cailiflower, peas and carrots to a pressure cooker or pot. Pour 1,5 cups water. Should be enough to cover them partially.
  4. Pressure cook for 1 to 2 whistles on medium flame.
  5. Veggies should be soft cooked. Mash them well.

to prepare onion tomato masala

  1. heat 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon oil in a pan.
  2. add 0,75 to 1 cup of chopped onions. Saute till translucent.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste and 1 green chilli. Fry until smells good. Don't burn, dummy
  4. Add half cup choppen bell pepper. Fry for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Add 0,75 to 1 cup chopped tomatoes and 1 teaspoon salt. Fry until tomatoes turn mushy, soft and pulpy.
  6. add teaspoon chilli power and 1 to 1,5 tablespoon pav bhaji masala powder.
  7. mix well and fry for 2 to 3 minutes.

make bhaji

  1. add the boiled and mashed veggies. Add 0,5 to 0,75 cups of water to bring it to a consistency.
  2. mix well and simmer for a while to bring the flavor of the masala. Taste test for salt, chili powder and pav bhaji masala.
  3. add 0,75 tablespoon kasuri methi. What is this?. When it reaches the desired consistency, add coriander leaves. Turn the FLAME off. Add some lemon juice before serving.

toast pav

  1. slit the pav horizontally. on a low flame, heat 1 teaspoon butter on a frying pan. add little masala powder over it.
  2. place pav for 1 to 2 min until slightly crisp.
  3. garnish with coriander leaves, lemon wedges and chopped onions. Top with more butter on hot bhaji. Server pav bhaji with more butter topped and onion.
  4. Enjoy!
