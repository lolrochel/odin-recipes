Wasabi green peas

Healthy, yummy homemade wasabi green peas with no additional starch and sugar.

ingredients

  • 2 cups peas, frozen
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon wasabi paste
  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder

steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â° F.
  2. Thaw frozen peas and let them rest on paper towels for about 15 min.
  3. Spread the peas in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with foil.
  4. Drizzle 1 tsp olive oil over peas and stir till well coated.
  5. Bake them for about 35-50 minutes.
  6. Stir every 10 min so that they all get roasted evenly.
  7. While peas are roasting, mix 1 tsp olive oil, wasabi paste and matcha together.
  8. When peas are roasted, remove from oven and coat with the matcha-wasabi paste. (The heat from the peas helps to soften the paste so peas get coated nicely.)
  9. Cool them completely and munch away!
